CityRunQuest

a mobile application that allows you to complete and create quests in AR at a selected location.

Idea

Using augmented reality technology to provide a unique user experience when completing and creating quests using your own phone.

Comfortable creation of quests for cultural and material benefits by local residents.

Opportunities of the application for the participant:

  • complete a quest and learn something new
  • get information about places around
  • providing information without feeling forced

Opportunities of the application for the local resident:

  • create a quest in AR between different locations
  • promote these places and partners around
  • creating quests is similar to work with Photoshop + Minecraft Earth
